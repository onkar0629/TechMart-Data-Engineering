
# Business Requirements

## Purpose

The TechMart project simulates a modern e-commerce platform for analytics and engineering practice.

## Functional Requirements

- Generate realistic customer, product, order, payment, and review data.
- Support reporting on sales, inventory, returns, and customer activity.
- Enable SQL and Python-based data engineering exercises.
- Prepare the data model for Azure and Power BI integration.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- Scalable and modular design
- Clear documentation
- Production-oriented naming and constraints
- Extensible for future business logic


# Data Dictionary

## Core Entities

### addresses
Stores customer and shipping address information.

### customers
Represents registered shoppers and their profile information.

### categories
Defines product grouping for navigation and analytics.

### brands
Stores product brand names and origin data.

### suppliers
Represents vendors that provide products to the platform.

### products
Represents the product catalog with pricing and SKU information.

### warehouses
Represents fulfillment centers where inventory is stored.

### inventory
Tracks stock quantities by product and warehouse.

### orders
Stores order-level information and total amount.

### order_items
Represents products included in each order.

### payments
Captures transaction details and payment method.

### reviews
Stores customer reviews and ratings.

### shipments
Tracks shipment progress and carrier events.

### returns
Documents product returns and refund details.

### employees
Stores warehouse and operations employee data.

### coupons
Represents discount coupons and their validity rules.

### wishlists
Captures products saved by customers for later purchase.

### shopping_cart
Represents customer shopping cart state.

### shopping_cart_items
Stores products inside each shopping cart.
